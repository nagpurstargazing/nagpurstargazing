About Nagpur Stargazing

Nagpur Stargazing is an astronomy outreach initiative based in Nagpur, Maharashtra, dedicated to making astronomy accessible, engaging and inspiring for everyone.

Founded by a team of passionate amateur astronomers and science communicators, we bridge the gap between textbooks and the real night sky through interactive learning and telescope observations, designed for students, teachers, families and anyone curious about the Universe.

Every session combines scientific knowledge with practical observation — allowing participants to experience celestial objects firsthand through professional-grade telescopes, from the Moon's craters to the rings of Saturn.

Founder's backgroundAstronomy & public science communication experience via Raman Science Centre and Planetarium, Nagpur.

Our Mission

six commitments that shape every session we run, from a single classroom to an open public evening.

01

To make astronomy enjoyable and accessible for people of all ages through interactive learning and telescope observations.

02

To foster scientific temper, critical thinking and a spirit of exploration among students and the general public.

03

To provide unforgettable hands-on experiences through guided telescope observations and practical astronomy activities.

05

To spread awareness about light pollution and promote the preservation of our natural night skies.

04

To help people reconnect with the beauty of the night sky and understand our place in the Universe..

06

To create memorable astronomy experiences that educate, inspire, and bring people together under the stars.
